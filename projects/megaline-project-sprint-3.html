Project Overview

This project analyzes two prepaid telecom plans, Surf and Ultimate, to determine which plan generates more revenue for Megaline. The analysis is based on data from 500 clients, focusing on their usage patterns in terms of calls, messages, and internet data.

Key Sections of the Analysis

  • Initialization: Importing necessary libraries for data analysis.
  • Data Loading: Reading data from CSV files into pandas DataFrames.
  • Data Preparation: Exploring and cleaning the data for analysis.
  • Data Enrichment: Adding additional columns for deeper insights.
  • User Behavior Study: Analyzing usage patterns of calls, messages, and internet data.
  • Revenue Analysis: Calculating and visualizing the revenue generated by each plan.
  • Statistical Hypothesis Testing: Testing for significant differences in revenue between plans and regions.
  • General Conclusion: Summarizing the findings and insights from the analysis.

Conclusion

The analysis revealed that the Surf plan generates more revenue on average compared to the Ultimate plan, primarily due to users frequently exceeding their plan limits. Statistical tests confirmed the significance of these findings.
